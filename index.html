• Holy Guacamole!

    How to make the best guacamole in town.

  • Ingredients

    The ratio is up to your personal preference. Hover over or tap each ingredient to find out more about each ingredient.









  • Step 1

    Mash

    Scoop out the avocado into the bowl and sprinkle salt over it. Then mash until it is creamy.

  • Step 2

    Bring Out The Knives

    Mince to tiny square pieces. Dice to slightly bigger cubes. Chop to thin, short slices.

  • Step 3

    Toss It All In

    Add the garlic, onion, jalapeno, cilantro, pepper, paprika, cayenne and cumin. Mix together.

  • Step 4

    Add Some Acidity

    Squeeze lemon and lime juice on top. Stir to incorporate it into the mixture.

  • Step 5

    Chill and Serve

    Chill 1 hour.
